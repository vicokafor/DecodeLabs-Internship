## **Project Overview**

This project explores sales performance using SQL queries to uncover trends, revenue drivers, product performance, customer behavior, and operational insights.
## **Tools Used**
- MySQL
- SQL
- GitHub
## **Analysis Performed**
- Total Revenue Analysis
- Product Performance
- Monthly Revenue Trends
- Payment Method Analysis
- Referral Source Analysis
- Order Status Analysis
- Business Insights & Recommendations
## **Key Findings**
- Chairs were the best-performing product, generating both the highest sales volume and highest revenue.
- Instagram was the strongest acquisition channel, contributing the highest number of orders and revenue.
- Online payments were the most popular payment method, while Credit Card transactions generated the highest revenue.
- Order cancellations represented a significant revenue risk and may be negatively impacting business performance.
- Customer spending patterns varied across payment methods, products, and referral sources, highlighting opportunities for targeted business strategies.
